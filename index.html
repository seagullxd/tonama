How to Play?

You're given a surname, for example:

"Smith"

You want to guess what country this surname originally comes from.

Say you guess "Wales". A card will then appear containing the country you guessed.

The value on the right hand side is the distance between the biggest cities of both your guess, Wales, and the correct country.

Use this distance as a guide to solve the puzzle. Keep guessing until you guess the correct country. In this example, the correct country is...

Helpful tips?

The distance between two countries is based on bilateral distances between the biggest cities of those two countries. For example, the distance between Cardiff, Wales and London, England is 180km.

Be mindful when trying to guess a country with a large land mass, e.g., China or Russia.

Make use of hints when you get stuck!
